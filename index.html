Rosemarie's Recommended Shows

The Good Doctor

This show generally revolves around a surgeon with down syndrom with an IQ that surpasses the average person. His condition makes him looked down upon and is constantly underestimated by his peers but continues to exceed their expectations.

How To Get Away With Murder

"How to Get Away with Murder" follows law professor Annalise Keating and her students as they become involved in a murder and its subsequent cover-up, delving into the complexities of the legal system and personal ethics.

Murder Mystery

In the Netflix movie "Murder Mystery," a couple's European vacation takes a thrilling turn when they get caught up in a murder investigation, leading to comedic chaos and unexpected revelations.

SUITS

"Suits" tracks Mike Ross, a college dropout, joining forces with lawyer Harvey Specter to handle cases despite Mike's lack of a law degree, showcasing their legal skills and dynamic partnership.
